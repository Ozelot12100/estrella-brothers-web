## Project Overview

We were called to assess and repair extensive wood rot damage on a Scottsdale home. The damage had spread from the fascia boards into the rafter tails and required structural replacement.

### What We Did
- Assessed full extent of rot damage
- Removed and replaced all affected rafter tails
- Installed new fascia and soffit boards
- Treated all adjacent wood with borate solution

### Materials
- Pressure-treated lumber for all exterior framing
- PVC fascia boards (rot-proof)
- Borate wood preservative treatment
